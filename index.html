Assemble


A new maker/creator/hacker/artist space in Scottsdale, Arizona

What

Welcome hackers, poets, coders, artists, musicians. Makers and creators.

Hacking around with an Arduino or Raspberry Pi? This is the place.

Using game controllers to drive MIDI devices? Come here.

Want to create sculpture using a 3D printer? Join us.

Need some space for that larger artwork? Drop by.

The goal is provide a place for smart people making cool stuff. People looking to explore ideas, meet people from different disciplines, and exchange ideas, techniques, and help each other.
